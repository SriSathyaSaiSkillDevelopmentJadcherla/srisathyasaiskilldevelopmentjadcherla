Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our programs and services.

You can enroll by visiting our centre during working hours, calling our enrollment hotline at +91 8555 287239, or filling out the online registration form on our website. Our staff will guide you through the process and help you choose the right program based on your interests and skills.

Most of our basic skill development programs are offered free of charge to economically disadvantaged students. Some advanced programs may have nominal fees to cover material costs. We also offer scholarships for deserving candidates. Please contact us for specific program fee details.

For registration, please bring:

  • Aadhaar Card or any government-issued ID proof
  • Proof of residence (ration card/electricity bill/etc.)
  • 2 passport-size photographs
  • Income certificate (if applying for free/subsidized programs)
  • Educational certificates (for certain programs)

Yes, we have tie-ups with several local businesses and industries for placement of our trained students. We conduct regular placement drives and also provide entrepreneurship support for those who wish to start their own small businesses. Our placement cell works continuously to create opportunities for our graduates.

We welcome both volunteers and donors to support our mission. Volunteers can help as trainers, mentors, or in administrative roles. Donations help us expand our programs and reach more beneficiaries. Please visit our 'Join Us' or 'Donate' pages for more information, or contact us directly to discuss how you can contribute.
