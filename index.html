Sri Sathya Sai Skill Development Centre

In affiliation with Sri Sathya Sai Seva Organizations, we offer free vocational training to deserving youth, delivered by experienced industry professionals in a modern, hands-on learning setting.

Skill Training

Gain real-world skills through industry-relevant, practical training.

Value Education

Guiding youth to develop strong values and practical skills for success in both life and career.

Placement Support

Supporting students in finding job opportunities after training.

Why Choose Our Centre?

  • An initiative of Sri Sathya Sai Seva Organizations, renowned for their excellence in value-based education.
  • Training programs offered at no cost to all eligible students.
  • Expert faculty with hands-on industry experience.
  • Modern classrooms and skill-based training infrastructure.
